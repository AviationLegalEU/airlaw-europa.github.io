About Runway 33

Our sky is European

Spanning from passengers to pilots, and from airports to the skies overhead, the European Union lies at the core of the regulatory framework that governs the aviation sector. The reason for Runway 33 is the firm belief in a fundamental principle : a commitment to adopting a dinstinctly European approach in providing knowledge about both national and European aviation law. This project is inspired by the collaborative efforts of the European Union towards a cohesive air transport policy. This is precisely why, within its primary objectives, this core principle and aspiration influences how Runway33 hopes to provide distinctive insights that can assist an international audience seeking relevant infomration.

Why the name "Runway 33"

From a firm belief in European cooperation in the sector of aviation, Runway 33 is a project that believes in sharing of European knowledge in the aviation sector.

A project driven by passion

As a personal project, Runway 33 is born out of a passion for aviation and the european collaboration governing aviation law. The author who has designed this website on his own freetime hopes that this passion is reflected in its content.

Word of the Author

It was very clear from the onset that two of my passions would lead me to aviation law : the love for aviation and the love for European law. This passion has lead me to create this website to provide this unique perpsective.

As an aviation lawyer at the Bar of Paris, I have witnessed that a lot information on aviation law is hardly accessible. When you try to find information related to national laws of Member States, it is still difficult to find it for an international audience. I created this website to bring a new light on this sector of law with unique insights from our twenty seven Member States including my own : France.

As a personal project solely motivated by passion, it is not intended to provide knowledge with the same quality and extensiveness as legaltechs and researchers. However, I will always link you to expert lawyers and scholars and articles for more information.
